Brownie Recipe

Here is a great, easy brownie recipe for beginner bakers!

Ingredients

  • 3 Medium Eggs
  • 250g Dark Chocolate
  • 150g Sugar
  • 100g Plain White Flour
  • 50g Milk Chocolate
Method:
  1. melt both dark and milk chocolate in a pot
  2. whisk three eggs and combine with chocolate
  3. fold in flour and sugar
  4. bake at 200c/300f for 25/30 mins
  5. Enjoy!
