Brownie Recipe

A tasty and easy-to-grasp meringue recipe for absolute bakers!

Ingredients

  • 5 Egg whites
  • 100g Icing sugar
  • 115g Caster sugar
Method:
  1. Heat the oven to 110C and line 2 baking sheets with parchment/greaseproof paper.
  2. whisk all the egg white until peaks form. Slowly add in sugar,one spoon at a time.
  3. Make sure to not overmix. Then, pour contents of fist-sized mixture onto baking sheets.
  4. bake in your preheatedoven for 80-100 minutes.
  5. Leave to cool. When cooled, enjoy!
